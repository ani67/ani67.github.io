BIXBY

interaction design, voice intelligence platform, research, UI/UX, Samsung R&D Bangalore

Firstly, I am sorry as I cannot really show any screens related to much of my work in Samsung due to NDA. However, what I can do is talk about my role and experience. After graduating from IIT Guwahati, I joined Samsung in their R&D center in Bangalore in the design team of their voice intelligence platform. 

As a designer, my main role in the team was to work closely with the Korean team on Bixby. Bixby is a smart voice assistant and we worked on various design aspects of Bixby for both the mobile and the watch. Voice assistants are new and they have their fair share of challenges assosciated with them. Initially, I worked on the UX for Bixby on the Galaxy Watch. Creating and maintaining the user experience documentation formed a major part of the work as we had to constantly update and maintain the guides in accordance with the ongoing development.

Apart from the design and maintainence of design guides, my work involved contributing to Bibxy event graphics for the US market. Based off of the Samsung Bixby visual style, we created a design language and a series of illustrations for various events for the US and global market.
Moreover, I worked on Bixby Chat, researching and creating proposals for new features for the US and global market.

It was an awesome experience to be able to work in a great team on a very innovative and cutting edge product in the global market and it provided me with a great experience of working in a diverse team across borders and domains.
