Bixby

Interaction / Visual Design / Research / *NDA

I was a part of the Voice Intelligence design team at Samsung R&D Bangalore.

During my stay, I worked on Bixby User Guidelines for the Samsung Galaxy watch. The role involved creating user experience for Bixby for use on the watch, maintaining active documentation with the ongoing development, working in close colaboration with the Bixby teams in US and South Korea, raising strings for UX writing and textual database, and creating proposals for new features.

The work occasionally involved creating Bibxy event graphics for the US market. Based off of the Samsung Bixby visual style, we created a design language and a series of illustrations for various events, like Christmas or Father's Day for example. Proposals were created for mixed paradigm usage while communicating with a voice interface as well as visually through a device, mobile/watch.

Moreover, the work involved occasional research work involving surveys and user testing to create proposals for new voice features and proposals for Bixby Chat experience, for handling conversations that don't trigger third party integrations.

It was an awesome experience to be able to work in a great team on a very innovative and cutting edge product in the global market and it provided me with a great experience of working in a diverse team across borders and domains
