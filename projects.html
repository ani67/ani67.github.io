Veha | research, cartography, data visualisation, landscape, biodiversity, Strelka Institute
A speculative design proposal that reimagines the planetary in the context of E O Wilson's Half Earth thesis. Set in the context of Russia, this project starts from the pragmatic, as a speculative proposal towards planetary viability based on the reimagination of planetarity with a shift in our attitude towards nature and preserving existing biodiversity as a Terraforming tool.

Anatomy of a Trojan Horse | research, media, planetary metabolism, fake news, trophic cascades, Strelka Institute
A research project that looks at media as a Terraforming tool in the grand scope of a vast planetary metabolism network. The project reveals with the help of case studies how exactly a simple fake message might result in a series of trophic cascades affecting other aspects in our current artificial metabolism.

Bixby | interaction design, voice intelligence platform, research, UI/UX, Samsung R&D Bangalore
Bixby is the voice intelligence platform of Samsung. As a part of the Bixby Design team in Bangalore, I worked on the user experience for Bixby on the Samsung Active watch for the global market, multiple research projects and project proposals for new features and Bixby Chat for the US market. 

Emergence of Data | data visualization, sound, interface, game, information, art, IIT Guwahati
Emergence of Data deals with the dichotomy created by the existence of agency in relation to any data source. When not guided by any definite problem statement, but rather a holistic top down extrapolation, the data reveals new scope for future possibilities made evident by the use of realtime sound in context of this project. Resulting in sound as art, information and interface.

Chronicles | design methodology, interaction design, collaboration, literature, Ozchi 24 hour challenge
Chronicles is a collaborative writing application. With a focus on kids and classroom environments, this project aims to foster the practise of creative writing among kids and people interested in literature as a collaborative endeavour. 

Shunya | theory of everything, platform, science, education, game, interaction, IIT Guwahati
Shunya is a framework for everything. Our understanding of the universe and our own origin has advanced quite a bit in our own lifetime. This framework is aimed at both scientists and students as a collaborative effort in order to simulate and experiment with various aspects of our understanding as a scientific gamified platform.

Oceans | visual design, symmetry, surrealism, abstract
Oceans is a series of manipulated images as an abstraction of reality, creating newer dimensions.
