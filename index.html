PROJECTS

STRELKA

Postgraduate researcher in the Terraforming 2020 program at Strelka, working on speculative design futures. (Ongoing)

BIXBY

As a part of the Design team at Samsung, I worked on UX design guidelines for Bixby on watch and Bixby Chat for the mobile specific to the US market.

DATA STORIES

The problem behind solving problems is the creation of new problems resulting from the isolation of an object from its network and the myopic study, directed by its agenda subject to varying human constructs. This is a macroscopic study, an investigation of sorts resulting in the emergence of multiple possibilities. (Masters Thesis Project)

EDIFY

A modular wearable health monitoring device for soldiers in high altitudes. It is an appendage for passive monitoring and data gathering in order to analyse what the soldiers are exposed to at extreme conditions. 

CHRONICLES

A shared texting platform for smart classrooms inspired from the Exquisite Corpse surrealist parlour game as an entry in a 24 hour design challenge with 'sharing' as the core theme.

ASSEMBLY OF DESIRE

Worked on various segments from documentation to production as a part of the Assembly of Desire festival, which is a multidisciplinary symposium and festival initiated by desire machine collective. As a socially engaged project, which is site-specific and event-driven, the project Assembly which is located on the Majuli island will engage with the very processes of living in the space, and attempt to blur the line between art and life.
 

SHUNYA

Shunya is an educative game as a generative framework where the player explores the history of the universe as we believe it to be. Drawing parallels between spiritual and scientific beliefs as our body as a biological machine paired up with energy(Life force) as the soul, this game revolves around the dual nature of all things around us. The player starts as a tiny particle lost in the cosmos interacting with different objects in the vicinity to transform into other objects setting in motion a chain reaction with endless possibilities.
